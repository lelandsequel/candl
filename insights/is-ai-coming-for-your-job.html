Is AI coming for your job?

Workforce & AI September 28, 2025

Short answer: It depends on the task, not the title. AI replaces repetitive judgment and accelerates research, but it amplifies domain experts who learn to wield it.

What gets automated

  • High-volume classification and routing (emails, tickets, forms)
  • First drafts of commodity content and summaries
  • Data reconciliation and simple cross-checks

What gets supercharged

  • Analysts who prompt against internal data and tools
  • Advisors who pair AI research with human context and judgment
  • Operators who use copilots to reduce errors and cycle times

How to future-proof

  • Map tasks to: Automate, Augment, or Avoid (re-skill)
  • Build a personal stack: retrieval over your docs, agentic checklists
  • Track impact: speed, quality, and scope expansion

Five AI‑proof trade businesses you can start this week for $1,000 or less

Small Business September 20, 2025

Local, physical, relationship‑driven work is hard to automate. Here are five service businesses you can start lean and grow profitably.

1) Mobile pressure washing

  • Startup: pressure washer, hose reels, basic PPE
  • Playbook: route density; HOA & small commercial

2) Gutter cleaning & guards

  • Startup: ladders, safety gear, hand tools
  • Playbook: seasonal packages; upsell guards

3) Dryer vent & air duct cleaning

  • Startup: vac, brushes, inspection camera (optional)
  • Playbook: safety angle; partner with property managers

4) Window cleaning

  • Startup: poles, squeegees, pure water system (optional)
  • Playbook: storefront routes; quarterly plans

5) Handyman “punch list” service

  • Startup: core tools; insured
  • Playbook: fixed‑scope bundles; transparent pricing

Market with simple before/after photos, direct response offers, and fast follow‑up. Keep it tight; scale what works.

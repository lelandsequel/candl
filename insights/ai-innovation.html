Kindling AI Innovation: Beyond the Hype to Real Business Value

AI Implementation September 10, 2025

AI is a tool, not a strategy. Real value appears when you weave AI into the fabric of work—specifically where delays, handoffs, and errors currently live. Start small, then scale.

Choose the Right First Use Case

  • Pick a narrow, high-friction step (e.g., case triage, invoice matching)
  • Define the outcome metric upfront (cycle time, error rate, cost per case)
  • Design for human-in-the-loop to maintain quality during rollout

Prove ROI in 90 Days

  • Baseline the process today with 2–3 metrics
  • Automate the step; measure weekly against baseline
  • Publish a one-page scoreboard for stakeholders

Scale with Guardrails

Once ROI is proven, scale to adjacent steps. Add governance: prompt/version control, audit logging for regulated use cases, and access controls tied to data sensitivity.
